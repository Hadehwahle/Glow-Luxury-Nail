Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us directly at 09131765834, sending us a WhatsApp message, or filling out the contact form above. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We welcome walk-ins based on availability, but we highly recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get your preferred time slot and nail technician.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and mobile payments. Payment is typically taken at the end of your service.

What if I need to cancel my appointment?

We understand that plans change. We appreciate at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. For cancellations with less than 24 hours notice, a small cancellation fee may apply.
