Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a gel manicure last?

Our gel manicures typically last 2-3 weeks with proper care. To extend the life of your gel manicure, wear gloves when cleaning, apply cuticle oil daily, and avoid using your nails as tools.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We welcome walk-ins based on availability, but we highly recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get your preferred time slot and nail technician. You can book online or call us directly.

What safety measures do you take for hygiene?

We prioritize your health and safety. All our tools are properly sterilized between clients, we use disposable items when possible, and our pedicure bowls have disposable liners. Our staff follows strict hygiene protocols including regular hand washing and sanitizing.

Can I bring my own nail polish?

Yes, you're welcome to bring your own polish. However, we carry an extensive collection of premium polishes, including vegan and 10-free options in a wide range of colors.

Do you offer nail services for special occasions?

Absolutely! We offer special packages for weddings, proms, and other events. For group bookings, please contact us at least two weeks in advance so we can accommodate your party. We also offer private parties with complimentary champagne for groups of 5 or more.
